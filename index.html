Introduction

I am a Ph.D student in Biostatistics at University of Pittsburgh working with Dr. Jong-Hyeon Jeong. My main research area has been time-to-event data analysis, specifically, deep learning for survial data.

I have also been working as Graduate Student Researcher at MYHAT group at UPMC.

I am currently working as a Biostatistician focusing on mRNA research at Translational and Early Development Biostatistics Department at Sanofi Pasteur.
